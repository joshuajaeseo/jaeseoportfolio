Design Thinking Communication


Our client from Byline Bank came to us with the challenge of overhauling her approach to teaching her financial literacy class. While the program had been successful when taught in-person, our client was finding it difficult to keep the same level of engagement when the pandemic forced her lessons to be taught virtually.

After interviews and secondary research, our team pinpointed several requirements: accessibility for those with limited access to technology, an interactive and engaging experience, and consideration for different learning styles.
